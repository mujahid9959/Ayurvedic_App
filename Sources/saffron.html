Overview

Saffron, derived from the flower *Crocus sativus*, is one of the most valuable spices in the world. Known as "red gold," saffron is used in culinary, medicinal, and cosmetic applications.

Benefits

    Rich in Antioxidants

  • Contains crocin and safranal, which protect cells from damage and reduce inflammation.

    • Improves Mood

  • Helps combat depression and anxiety by boosting serotonin levels.

    • Supports Eye Health

  • Enhances vision and protects against age-related macular degeneration (AMD).

    • Heart Health

  • Reduces cholesterol and improves blood flow.

    • Skin Benefits

  • Brightens complexion, reduces acne, and promotes healthy skin.

    • Weight Management

  • Curbs appetite and reduces emotional eating.

Uses

  • Culinary dishes for flavor, aroma, and color.
  • Traditional medicine to treat mood disorders, digestion issues, and menstrual problems.
  • Skincare for brightening and soothing properties.
  • Fragrance and dye production.

Number of Plants

Turmeric is grown from rhizomes (not seeds). The number of plants depends on spacing and rhizome weight.

  • Spacing: 1.5 ft x 1 ft (common practice)
  • Plants per Acre: ~29,000 to 30,000 rhizomes
  • Plants per Acre=Area in Square Feet/Plant Spacing=43,560/1.5×1=29,040

Cost Breakdown

Rhizomes for Planting:

  • Rhizome Requirement: ~800–1,000 kg per acre
  • Cost per kg: ₹30–₹50
  • Total Cost of Rhizomes: ₹24,000–₹50,000

    • Land Preparation:

  • Land clearing, plowing, and bed preparation: ₹10,000–₹20,000

    • Manure and Fertilizers:

  • Organic manure: 8–10 tons per acre (₹1,000–₹1,500 per ton)
  • Fertilizer: ~₹5,000–₹8,000
  • Total Cost for Manure and Fertilizer: ₹13,000–₹18,000

    • Irrigation Setup:

  • Turmeric requires consistent watering.
  • Drip Irrigation: ₹30,000–₹50,000 (one-time cost)

    • Labor Costs:

  • Planting, weeding, and initial maintenance: ₹10,000–₹15,000

Maintenance Costs (Yearly)

  • Weeding and Irrigation: ₹5,000–₹10,000
  • Pest and Disease Management: ₹2,000–₹5,000

Yield and Revenue

  • Time to Harvest: 7–9 months.
  • Yield per Acre: 8–10 tons (depending on soil and care).
  • Market Price: ₹2,500–₹4,000 per quintal (₹25–₹40 per kg).
